Staying Safe

Travel Planning

Once you've chosen a destination, it's time to start planning your trip. Here are a few things to keep in mind: Budget: Set a budget for your trip and stick to it. Transportation: How will you get to and from your destination? Will you need to book flights, trains, or buses? Accommodation: Where will you stay? Will you book a hotel, hostel, Airbnb, or something else? Activities: What do you want to see and do on your trip? Make a list of attractions and activities before you go.
